# Bitcoin Western Rider 🤠₿

**A live Bitcoin price visualization where market movement becomes a
Western rollercoaster trail.**

Bitcoin Western Rider transforms live BTC price activity into an
animated ride through a Western landscape. Short-term price changes
create bumps and dips, sustained momentum creates longer climbs and
descents, and the Bitcoin rider follows and tilts with the terrain.

> **Best experienced on a desktop or laptop browser.**

## 🌵 Live Demo

**[View Bitcoin Western
Rider](https://j4ykl.github.io/bitcoin-western-rider/)**

## ✨ Features

-   Live Bitcoin price data from Coinbase
-   Coinbase WebSocket connection with REST fallback
-   Pronounced terrain bumps based on short-term BTC price movement
-   Longer uphill and downhill slopes based on sustained price momentum
-   Gradual trail leveling during quieter market movement
-   Upper and lower terrain limits to help prevent extreme cliffs
-   Bitcoin rider that follows and tilts with the terrain
-   Faster scrolling trail for a rollercoaster-style experience
-   Daily Bitcoin mountain generated from 15-minute price data
-   Daily mountain peak price label
-   Bull, bear, and quiet trail status
-   Animated Western scenery
-   Automatic daily mountain refresh every 15 minutes
-   Browser visibility handling to reduce unnecessary activity in
    background tabs

## 🎢 How It Works

The incoming trail combines two types of Bitcoin movement:

1.  **Short-term price movement** creates the smaller bumps and dips.
2.  **Sustained momentum** creates longer directional climbs and
    descents.

When price movement becomes quiet, the trail gradually levels. The rider
continuously follows the terrain and leans backward while climbing and
forward while descending.

## 🚀 Running Locally

Download or clone the repository, then open `index.html` in a modern web
browser.

For the best experience, use a desktop or laptop browser with an active
internet connection so the live Coinbase price feed can load.

## 📱 Mobile

The project can open on mobile devices, but the full Western landscape
and rollercoaster effect are designed primarily for wider desktop and
laptop screens.

## 🔒 Privacy

This project uses public Coinbase market-data services. It does not
require visitors to sign in or provide personal information.
